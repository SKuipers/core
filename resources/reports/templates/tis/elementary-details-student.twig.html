Dispositions
In the TIS ECE program we support the children in their development of key learning dispositions and assess their progress in their development. The development of appropriate dispositions is fundamental to a young child’s future learning and well-being. The notion that children learn every day through every situation is a central idea in Flight: Alberta’s Early Learning and Care Framework. Using the framework Flight, we identify five dispositions to learn: playing and playfulness, seeking,participating, persisting, and caring. This report card provides insight into the observed development of your child’s dispositions in regards to these five areas.
